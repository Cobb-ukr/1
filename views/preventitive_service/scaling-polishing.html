Scaling and Polishing

What it is: A professional cleaning procedure where plaque and tartar (hardened plaque) are removed from teeth, particularly below the gumline. Polishing follows to smooth the surfaces and remove surface stains.

Why it's needed: Scaling is often recommended when there's significant plaque buildup or gum disease (gingivitis or periodontitis). Regular scaling helps prevent cavities and gum disease by removing the substances that contribute to them.
