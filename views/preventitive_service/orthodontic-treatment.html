Orthodontic Treatment

What it is: A range of treatments designed to correct misaligned teeth and jaws.

  • Braces: Metal or ceramic brackets connected by wires gradually shift teeth into proper alignment.
  • Clear Aligners (e.g., Invisalign): Transparent plastic trays that align teeth without the visibility of traditional braces.
  • Retainers: Worn after braces to maintain the new position of teeth.

Why it's needed: To improve bite function, prevent wear or damage to teeth, enhance speech and chewing, and improve the overall aesthetics of the smile. Correcting alignment can also reduce the risk of gum disease and cavities.
